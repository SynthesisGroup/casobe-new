Frequently Asked Questions

Whats is Casobe

Calatagan South Beach (CaSoBē), the latest Leisure Tourism Estate  (LTE) development of Landco nestled in the sands of Calatagan, Batangas.

This 15-hectares development is a master-planned leisure community open to beach home buyers, businessmen, tourists and travelers.

It offers investors the opportunity to build commercial establishments or live inside a beach resort with the convenience of hotel amenities, facilities and services

Where is Casobe

CaSoBē is approximately 3 hours driving distance from Metro Manila and 1.5 hours away from Tagaytay City. It is also accessible via the Manila-Cavite Expressway (CAVITEX) and Star Tollway via Lipa City. The proposed Cavite-Tagaytay-Batangas Expressway (CTBEX) shall reduce travel time by approximately 1 hour once completed

Whats in and around Casobe

Once known as Hacienda de Calatagan, the town is acquired by Don Domingo Roxas from the Spanish Crown in 1829, and has retained its old Spanish provincial charm and laidback ambiance.

CaSoBe complements this rich history by providing relaxation and entertainment to its community. It is accessible to exciting diving sites on the Verde Island Passage where the Underwater Pyramid can be found as well as Starfish island and ’Ang Pulo’ which offers Eco-tourism activities. They can also get a glimpse of the 18th century Lighthouse at Cape Santiago.

For those looking for thrilling adventures, there’s wakeboarding at Lago de Oro, while corporate executives may close business deals while playing Golf at Punta Baluarte.

Within CaSoBe, special attractions and amenities are already operational or soon to be operational to serve the residents and the tourist market.

What are Harbour Estates

Harbour Estates are lots offered for sale within Casobe. It is classified as commercial or mixed use where lot use can be for residential or commercial or both.

Why should I buy in Casobe

For the leisure investor, apart from making a sound real estate investment, It also establishes ones stature given that its more than owning a beach property, its owning a beach property in a thriving resort setting.

As property owners they can put up a beach house and add pocket businesses catering to the tourist traffic such as coffee shops and retail outlets.

But for those wanting to make a go at resort investments commercially, like hoteliers, then Casobe is the ideal place to start this endeavor.

Apart from 24/7 Security, the estate will also be provided with support services covering both residential and commercial needs of its property owners.

What are the other benefits of buying a lot at harbour estates

CaSoBē lot owners are fully entitled to the Harbour Estates’ hotel services and a gated, pedestrian and bicycle friendly community that is open to the public.
