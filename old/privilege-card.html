Updated Perks and Benefits of Privilege Club Members

Landco Privilege Club is an exclusive recognition and appreciation program for our valued clients. These are Landco customers who have successfully bought & completed the full purchase of any landco property.

Landco values its loyal customers, so in return we are providing them with exclusive discounts & access to our different developments around the Philippines.

What are the requirements to avail these discounts?

  • The guest must be a Landco Privilege Club Member
  • The club member must coordinate their booking and reservation through the Landco Customer Service Hotline
  • The club member must show their Landco Privilege Club Card upon check-in
How to become a club member?
  • A person must purchase a property at any Landco project. He/she is required to fully pay the property including the transfer fee charges to be enrolled to the Landco Privilege Club.
  • For the 2nd Owners/Buyers of Landco Properties, the person must provide a copy of the transferred property title, 2 valid IDs and settle the P500.00 membership enrollment fee to join the club.
