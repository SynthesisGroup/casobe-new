Welcome to Calatagan South Beach (CaSoBē)

The latest Leisure Tourism Estate (LTE) development of Landco on the shores of Batangas.

Nestled in the sands of Calatagan, Batangas, this 15-hectare development will set new grounds in Philippine Tourism and will redefine what life really should be. CaSoBē will host commercial establishments like hotels, condotels, condominiums, event’s place, bars and restaurants, retails shops, resort attractions and facilities, service-oriented shops and more
